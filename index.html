About The Event

About The Event

The Future of the Industry Entertainment Summit is a two-day event powered by Los Angeles Urban League Young Professionals will empower and educate college students and young professionals on how emerging technologies will impact future Film/TV, Music, and Sports/e-Sports.

The weekend will conclude with an intimate brunch to award current and future changemakers in Hollywood.

Danielle King, LAULYP President and Jasmine Kent, LAULYP Entertainment Chair

Opening Address

9:30 - 10:15am

Hack on Diversity in Entertainment

10:30 - 11:00am

A conversation between Entertainment Reps on the importance of including professionals of color on creative teams. Attendees will walk away insights on how to use transferable skills to gain employment, develop projects, and how Talent Managers, Lawyers, Publicists, and Agents work together to develop a project.

Facing Digitalization: The Future of Streaming

11:00 - 11:30am

As video and music streaming services continue to emerge, how will audiences find the platform that best fits their needs? Panelist will discuss how the segmentation of streaming services impacts inclusive storytelling across global media.

Side Hustle to CEO: Power and Profit

11:45 - 12:30pm

A panel of multi-hyphenates will share lessons on their journey in Entertainment. Creative Entrepreneurs will learn how to leverage the relationships and resources of their full- time job to fulfil the needs of a startup business, how to balance multiple projects, asking for what you’re worth, and finding your “Why” in the Industry.

Physical and Mental Health in Entertainment

2:30 - 3:00pm

Attendees will learn how to identify and find solutions to unhealthy practices around the high stakes of the Entertainment Industry. Future Executives, Artists, Influencers, and Entrepreneurs will learn how to protect their mental health in the age of social media and cope with the realities of the world.

The Future of Sports: AR/VR, AI, and 5G

3:00 - 3:30

Black leaders in STEAM have a conversation on the rise of AR/ VR, Artificial Intelligence, and how 5G will leverage these technologies to create personalized content and experiences in Sports and Gaming.
