About The Event

ABOUT THE EVENT

“The Future of the Industry” summit is a two-day event powered by Los Angeles Urban League Young Professionals that will empower and educate college students and young professionals on the emerging technologies that will impact the future of Film/TV, Music, and Sports/eSports.

The weekend will conclude with an intimate brunch and silent auction to award current and future changemakers in Hollywood. Silent auction proceeds will benefit LAULYP member-only events and the United Negro College Fund and its Emerging Leaders program.

Bridging the Gap: Creative Artists Agency (CAA) Presents the Business of Entertainment

10:00am - 11:00am

Positioned at the nexus of talent, content, brands, technology, sports, and live events, Creative Artists Agency (CAA) creates limitless opportunities for the storytellers, trendsetters, icons, and thought leaders who shape popular culture. Since our founding in 1975, across film, TV, music, sports, digital media, marketing, and beyond, CAA represents thousands of the world’s leading actors, directors, writers, producers, musical artists, comedians, authors, athletes, coaches, broadcasters, teams, leagues, chefs, designers, consumer brands, and more. Fostering an environment inclusive of more diverse voices, from diverse backgrounds, makes our company stronger, and provides a richer and better experience for our employees, our clients, and audiences worldwide.

Film Screening and Conversation: Colors of Sankofa

1:30-2:30pm

A few weeks before a trip to Ghana, budding CEO, Elyse Bempong finds herself forced to replace her second in command. After swimming through what seems like a never ending pile of applicant resumés, a familiar name appears in the pile. However, this feeling was far from warm and fuzzy. An undesired opening in her empire leads Elyse to a place she never thought she would have to go back to. A despised past she worked diligently to disappear returns in the most unlikely of ways.


This short drama was created to highlight the harsh realities of colorism in a world obsessed with lighter skin, as well as, dive into the long term effects of bullying.

In Partnership with UNCF Emerging Leaders


The Emerging Leaders is a group of young leaders in Los Angeles who are committed to UNCF’s mission, inspired by the opportunities, and eager to make an impact. Whether in their companies, among their peer groups, or in their community and civic leadership roles, Emerging Leaders take a forward-thinking approach to social impact and lead innovation in their programming and among their social circles. They come from every industry and every walk of life, but join together as a generation dedicated to carrying on the mission and the message of UNCF in Los Angeles."
